Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

"ember.finance was built around anonymity. We believe people share more honestly — and heal more openly — when they don't have to worry about being identified. We do not ask for your name, email address, or any account credentials. We will never sell your data."

Note: This is a template for informational purposes. Before publishing, have a qualified attorney review it for your jurisdiction, particularly if you expect users from the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA).
01

Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to anyone who visits or uses ember.finance, including people who read stories, post stories, or leave comments.

02

Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

ember.finance does not ask you to provide any personal information. When you submit a story or comment, you are not asked for your name, email, employer, or any identifying details. The only data associated with a submitted story is:

  • The content of the story itself
  • Your selected role category, years of experience, and burnout stage (all anonymised)
  • A randomly generated token (e.g. EMB-4X7K) — generated by your browser, not linked to your identity

2.2 Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our servers may automatically receive standard technical information when you visit, including your IP address (anonymised where possible), browser type and version, operating system, and pages visited. This is used solely for security monitoring and platform performance.

2.3 Cookies and tracking

ember.finance uses minimal cookies necessary to operate the site. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics tools that profile individual users.

03

How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Display stories and comments on the platform
  • Monitor for abuse, spam, or content that violates our Terms of Service
  • Understand general usage patterns to improve the platform (aggregated only)
  • Respond to content removal requests submitted with a valid story token

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

04

Story Tokens

Your token is the only way to locate and manage your story. It is not linked to your identity in any way and cannot be used by us to identify who you are. You are responsible for keeping it safe — if you lose it, we have no way to verify your ownership of a story.
05

Content You Submit

Stories and comments you post are visible to all visitors of ember.finance. Although no personal information is required, please be mindful of any details within your story that could inadvertently identify you (such as specific deals, dates, or firm characteristics).

If you wish to have your story removed, email hello@emberfinstories.com with your story token. We will process valid requests within 14 days.

06

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties. We may share limited technical data with:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers — to operate and maintain the platform, under contractual data protection obligations
  • Law enforcement or regulators — only if required by law or to prevent imminent harm

We will always seek to share the minimum information necessary.

07

Data Retention

User-submitted stories and comments are retained for as long as the platform is active, or until a removal request is submitted. Anonymised server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes.

08

Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the platform and its content, including HTTPS encryption and access controls. However, no system is completely secure. Please do not submit information to this platform that you would consider highly sensitive beyond what is needed to share your story.

09

Children's Privacy

ember.finance is not directed at anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted content to the platform, please contact us at hello@emberfinstories.com and we will remove it promptly.

10

International Users

ember.finance is operated from the State of New York, United States. If you are accessing the platform from outside this jurisdiction — including from the European Union or United Kingdom — please be aware that your information may be processed in a different country.

For EU/UK users (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing limited technical data is legitimate interest. You have the right to access, correct, or erase any personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, contact hello@emberfinstories.com.

For California users (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of your data. We do not sell personal data.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12

Contact

For privacy-related questions, data removal requests, or concerns — we aim to respond within 5 business days.
